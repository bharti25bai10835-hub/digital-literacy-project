## Project Overview
This repository contains my **Digital Literacy Portfolio Project** created as part of the Digital Literacy course at VIT Bhopal University.  
The goal of this project is to demonstrate awareness and practical understanding of digital literacy concepts such as safe internet practices, professional online presence, responsible communication, and cybersecurity awareness.

The project is divided into five tasks, each focusing on a different aspect of digital literacy.

---

## Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
A digital literacy awareness infographic was created to explain the importance of digital skills in the modern world.

The infographic covers:
- What digital literacy means
- Safe internet practices
- Professional online presence

## Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
A case study on **Phishing Cybercrime** was created along with a **Cybercrime Prevention Checklist** to raise awareness about online threats and safe digital practices.

The checklist includes important safety measures such as:
- Avoiding suspicious links
- Protecting OTPs and passwords
- Being cautious with UPI transactions
- Reporting cybercrime through official channels
